Zootopia Food Trucking

You are running a food truck in the bountiful city of Zootopia, with many parks and citizens. Your goal is to create the most amount of profit in a day. At the end of each hour, you can decide to move to a different park or stay at your current one. Then you can tally up your profit and use that alongside other information to decide your action for the next hour. Good luck!
